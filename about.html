MY NAME IS NICK MILANI

and I am obsessed with being my own boss, living life on my own terms, and enjoying the freedom it gives me.

My story begins when I finished my Engineering undergraduate degree. Something about getting a job and working my way up just didn't sit right with me. I decided to get my MEng in Innovation and Entrepreneurship to allow for more experimentation before I entered the "real world". After experimenting with stocks, coding, a service-based business, I found e-commerce -- as soon as I got into it, I knew that this was what I wanted to commit to, so I went all-in.

I further specialized with Amazon about a year into it and have never looked back. I currently run and operate a PL brand and a Wholesale business on Amazon. This allows me to live the life I want, work from anywhere in the world, and enjoy my time with the people I care most about. Now, this didn't happen overnight, it took a lot of time, money, and learning, but most importantly, I fell in love with the process, which is more important than just loving the outcome it can bring.

Outside of my businesses, I want to help individuals who were once in my position and/or need course-correction to grow quicker. Feel free to connect with me and say Hi!
